Seeds

Don’t underestimate their size — Seeds from Vinsark Foods are nature’s tiny powerhouses packed with energy, essential nutrients, and wholesome goodness. From heart-healthy omega-3s to immunity-boosting antioxidants, every spoonful is a step towards better health.

Just a spoonful a day can transform your meals — adding crunch, flavour, and nutrition to smoothies, salads, yoghurts, chapatis, baked goods, or even your morning cereal. They are versatile, easy to use, and suitable for all ages, making them a smart choice for everyday wellness.
